Milestone 5: Advancements in Computer Vision

Advancements in computer vision technologies in the 2000s have revolutionized various industries and applications. Computer vision systems have become adept at understanding and interpreting visual data, enabling tasks such as object recognition, image classification, and scene understanding. These advancements have led to significant progress in fields like autonomous vehicles, medical imaging, and augmented reality.
