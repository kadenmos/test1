Milestone 1: Invention of Turing Machine

The invention of the Turing Machine by Alan Turing in 1936 marked a significant milestone in the field of computer science and artificial intelligence. The Turing Machine laid the theoretical foundation for modern computing by introducing the concept of a universal computing machine capable of performing any computation that can be described algorithmically.
