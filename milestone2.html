Milestone 2: Birth of Neural Networks

The birth of neural networks in 1951 marked a significant milestone in artificial intelligence. The first artificial neural network model, the perceptron, was developed by Frank Rosenblatt. This early work laid the foundation for the field of deep learning, leading to advancements in pattern recognition, classification, and other cognitive tasks.
