Milestone 4: Breakthroughs in Natural Language Processing

Natural Language Processing (NLP) has achieved significant milestones over the years. From early rule-based systems to modern deep learning techniques, NLP has transformed the way computers understand and generate human language. Breakthroughs in machine translation, sentiment analysis, and question answering systems have made NLP an integral part of various applications, including virtual assistants, search engines, and language translation services.
